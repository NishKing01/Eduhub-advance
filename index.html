About EduHub

EduHub is designed for learners and makers who prefer hands-on learning over passive reading. Materials here are practical: annotated schematics, parts lists, step-by-step assembly guides, example code, and lab notes. Contributors are encouraged to include expected learning outcomes, estimated completion time, and safety notes where appropriate.
